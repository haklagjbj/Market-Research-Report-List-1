<p><p>The Plexiglass market consists of several key types: casting, injection, and extrusion. Casting involves creating sheets by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in high clarity and thickness options. Injection molding produces complex shapes by forcing molten acrylic into molds, ideal for intricate designs. Extrusion involves forcing acrylic through a die to form continuous sheets or shapes, suitable for large-scale production. Other markets may include applications like 3D printing or special formulations, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
