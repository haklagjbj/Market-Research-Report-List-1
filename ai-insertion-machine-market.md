<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AI Insertion Machine market can be categorized into two main types: radial and axial. Radial insertion machines place components in a circular pattern, suitable for high-density circuit boards, enhancing efficiency and speed. In contrast, axial insertion machines align components in a linear fashion, ideal for through-hole components, allowing for precise placement and greater control. Both types serve distinct manufacturing needs, providing flexibility and optimizing production processes in various electronic assembly applications.</p></p>
