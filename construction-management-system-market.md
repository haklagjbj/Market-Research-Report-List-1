<p><p>Construction management systems can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems are installed directly on the user's hardware, offering greater control and customization, but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and collaboration from various locations. This flexibility often results in lower initial costs and simpler updates, making cloud solutions increasingly popular among construction firms looking for efficient project management.</p></p>
