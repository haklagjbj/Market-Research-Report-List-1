<p><p>The Railway Automated Fare Collection (AFC) System encompasses three primary market types: fareboxes, ticket vending machines (TVMs), and validators. Fareboxes are secure units for collecting cash or card payments directly at stations or aboard trains. Ticket vending machines allow passengers to purchase tickets conveniently and usually support various payment methods. Validators serve to authenticate and validate tickets or cards before passengers board, enhancing security and streamlining the boarding process, ultimately improving overall efficiency in fare management.</p></p>
