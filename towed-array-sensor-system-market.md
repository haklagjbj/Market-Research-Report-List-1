<p><p>The Towed Array Sensor System market includes two primary types: passive sensors and active sensors. Passive sensors detect acoustic signals from submarines and underwater vessels without emitting their own signals, making them stealthy and suitable for surveillance. In contrast, active sensors emit sound waves and analyze the echoes that return, allowing for precise identification of targets. Each type serves distinct purposes in naval operations, with passive sensors prioritizing stealth and active sensors emphasizing target verification and range capability.</p></p>
