<p><p>The photo laminators market is primarily segmented into two types: pouch laminators and roll laminators. Pouch laminators utilize pre-cut plastic pouches to seal documents, making them ideal for home and small office use due to their user-friendly design. Conversely, roll laminators, which employ large rolls of laminate film, are suited for high-volume projects in commercial settings, offering faster production speeds and the ability to laminate larger materials. Both cater to diverse laminating needs, enhancing durability and presentation quality.</p></p>
