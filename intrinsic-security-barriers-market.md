<p><strong>The Intrinsic Security Barriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Zener Barriers</li><li>Galvanically Isolated Barriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intrinsic security barriers are essential in hazardous environments, designed to prevent ignition from electrical sources. Zener barriers utilize zener diodes to limit voltage and current, ensuring safe operation in explosive atmospheres. Galvanically isolated barriers, on the other hand, isolate input and output circuits, preventing fault currents from transmitting between them. Both types serve critical roles in process automation and safety, enabling reliable data transmission while minimizing the risk of ignition in flammable environments.</p></p>
