<p><p>Integrated Facility Management (IFM) encompasses various market types, primarily categorized as on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise IFM involves software and systems installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based IFM leverages online platforms, enabling remote access, scalability, and reduced costs due to lower infrastructure requirements. Each option caters to different organizational needs, reflecting their preferences for control, flexibility, and resource allocation.</p></p>
