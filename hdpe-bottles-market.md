<p><p>The HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) bottles market is characterized by two primary types: translucent and opaque. Translucent HDPE bottles allow some light to pass through, enabling visibility of the contents while providing moderate UV protection. They are often used for products like detergents and personal care items. Opaque HDPE bottles, on the other hand, block light completely, offering superior protection against UV radiation. This feature makes them ideal for storing chemicals, food products, and other sensitive materials, ensuring product integrity and longevity.</p></p>
