<p><p>The CMP PVA brush market is segmented into two primary types: roll and flake. Roll brushes are designed for efficient contouring and polishing, ideal for various surfaces and achieving precise finishes. They typically provide longer service life due to their continuous fiber arrangement. In contrast, flake brushes consist of loose fibers that offer a more aggressive cleaning action, suitable for removing stubborn contaminants. Both types serve distinct purposes in semiconductor manufacturing and fine polishing applications, enhancing process efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
