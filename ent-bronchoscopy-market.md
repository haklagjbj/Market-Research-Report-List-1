<p><p>The ENT bronchoscopy market is segmented into rigid and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes are solid, straight tubes designed for direct visualization and intervention in the airway, offering enhanced stability and access during procedures. In contrast, flexible endoscopes are more adaptable, featuring a thin, bendable design that allows for detailed examination of the respiratory tract while navigating around obstacles. Both types are crucial for diagnosing and treating various respiratory conditions, catering to different clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
