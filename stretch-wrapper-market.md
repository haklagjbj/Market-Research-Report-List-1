<p><p>The stretch wrapper market is categorized into three main types: Manual, Semiautomatic, and Automatic stretch wrappers. Manual stretch wrappers require an operator to wrap pallets by hand, offering flexibility and low initial costs. Semiautomatic models streamline the process with powered turntables or rotating arms, enhancing efficiency while still needing some manual input. Automatic stretch wrappers fully automate the wrapping process, improving throughput and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume operations. Each type caters to different operational needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
