<p><p>The natural gas storage service market comprises several key types. Above-ground storage typically utilizes large tanks, offering flexibility for short-term needs and peak demand. Floating natural gas storage refers to specialized vessels that store liquefied natural gas (LNG) offshore, enabling easy access to gas when needed while minimizing land use. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring gas availability and balancing market fluctuations.</p></p>
