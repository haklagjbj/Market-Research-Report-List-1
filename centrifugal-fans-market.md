<p><strong>The Centrifugal Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward</li><li>Backward</li><li>Radial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal fans are categorized into three main types based on their blade design: forward-curved, backward-curved, and radial. Forward-curved fans have blades that curve in the direction of rotation, offering high airflow at low pressure, ideal for HVAC systems. Backward-curved fans have blades that curve against the direction of rotation, providing higher efficiency and better pressure handling, suitable for industrial applications. Radial fans feature straight blades, generating significant pressure and airflow, commonly used in heavy-duty situations like dust collection and material handling.</p></p>
