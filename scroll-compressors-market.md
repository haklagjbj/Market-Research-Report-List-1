<p><p>Scroll compressors are a type of positive displacement compressor widely used in refrigeration and air conditioning applications. They operate using two spiral-shaped scroll elements, where one scroll remains stationary while the other orbits around it, compressing the refrigerant gas. This design enables high efficiency, low noise, and a compact size, making scroll compressors a preferred choice for various residential and commercial systems.</p><p>The Scroll Compressors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.90% during the forecast period.
