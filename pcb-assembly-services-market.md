<p><strong>The PCB Assembly Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Turnkey</li><li>Partial Turnkey</li><li>Kitted/Consigned</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB assembly services can be categorized into three main market types. Full turnkey services encompass everything from design to finished product, where the assembly provider manages all components and sourcing. Partial turnkey involves the assembly provider handling some components, while the client supplies others. Kitted or consigned services require the client to provide all components, and the assembly service focuses solely on manufacturing and assembling the PCBs. Each type offers different levels of client involvement and sourcing responsibility.</p></p>
