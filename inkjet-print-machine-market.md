<p><strong>The Inkjet Print Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Technology</li><li>Random Inkjet Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet print machine market is primarily categorized into Continuous Inkjet Technology and Random Inkjet Technology. Continuous Inkjet Technology utilizes a continuous stream of ink droplets, enabling high-speed printing suitable for large-scale industrial applications. In contrast, Random Inkjet Technology operates by ejecting ink droplets only when needed, allowing for more precise and variable printing, often used in smaller production runs or for specialized applications. Both technologies cater to different demands in quality, speed, and application versatility, shaping the market landscape.</p></p>
