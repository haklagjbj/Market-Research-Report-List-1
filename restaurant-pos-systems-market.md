<p><p>Restaurant POS systems come in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems operate online, allowing real-time data access from any location, facilitating easier updates and lower initial costs. They enhance scalability and integration with other online tools. On-premises systems, however, are installed locally and offer greater control over data and security, making them preferable for establishments with specific compliance needs. Each type suits different operational preferences and budget considerations, impacting efficiency and overall performance.</p></p>
