<p><strong>The Electrical Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Breaker</li><li>Main Lugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical panels market can be categorized primarily into two types: Main Breaker and Main Lugs. Main Breaker panels feature a built-in circuit breaker that protects the entire electrical system from overloads by interrupting power. In contrast, Main Lugs panels do not include a main circuit breaker; instead, they require an external breaker to manage power distribution. Each type serves distinct applications, with Main Breaker panels offering enhanced safety and Main Lugs panels providing flexibility for specific installations.</p></p>
