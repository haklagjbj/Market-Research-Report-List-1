<p><p>The industrial rotary air compressor market is primarily segmented into three types: screw, scroll, and others. Screw compressors utilize helical rotors to compress air efficiently, offering continuous operation and high reliability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Scroll compressors, on the other hand, consist of spiral-shaped components that compress air through orbital motion, providing quiet operation and compact design ideal for smaller applications. The "others" category encompasses various technologies such as vane and diaphragm compressors, catering to specialized needs.</p></p>
