<p><ul><li>LVP Drug</li><li>SVP Drug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parenteral drugs market is categorized into two main types: Large Volume Parenterals (LVP) and Small Volume Parenterals (SVP). LVP drugs typically include intravenous solutions administered in volumes greater than 500 mL, often used for hydration, nutrition, and medication delivery. Conversely, SVP drugs involve smaller volumes, typically less than 500 mL, and are used for concentrated medications, anesthesia, and injections. Both types are essential in healthcare, catering to diverse patient needs and treatment protocols.</p></p>
