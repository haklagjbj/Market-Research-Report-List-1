<p><p>The single-mode microplate readers market comprises specialized devices focused on distinct detection methods. Fluorescence plate readers measure emitted light from fluorescent samples, ideal for assays that require sensitivity and specificity. Absorbance plate readers quantify light absorption by samples, commonly used in colorimetric assays. Luminescence plate readers detect light produced by chemical reactions, perfect for measuring cellular activity and enzyme activity. Each type serves unique applications in life sciences, drug discovery, and clinical labs, enhancing research efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
