<p><ul><li>Redundant Distributed Control System</li><li>Non-redundant Distributed Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Computerized Distributed Control System (DCS) market can be divided into two main types: Redundant and Non-redundant Distributed Control Systems. Redundant DCS ensures high reliability by incorporating backup components that automatically take over in case of failures, making it ideal for critical applications in industries like oil and gas. Non-redundant DCS, while cost-effective, lacks this backup mechanism, making it suitable for less critical operations where downtime can be tolerated. Each type caters to specific industry requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
