<p><strong>The Posterior Spinal System Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uniaxial Screw</li><li>Polyaxial Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The posterior spinal system screws market includes two main types: uniaxial screws and polyaxial screws. Uniaxial screws allow for a single plane of motion, providing stability but limited flexibility in rod alignment. In contrast, polyaxial screws feature a multi-axial design, enabling dynamic positioning and improved rod application, which accommodates varying anatomical factors. The choice between these screw types impacts surgical outcomes, spinal stability, and the overall effectiveness of spinal fusion procedures. Each type has specific applications based on patient needs and surgical requirements.</p></p>
