<p><ul><li>Water Heating</li><li>Electric Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heating and cooling ceiling systems are designed to provide comfortable indoor climates through efficient temperature regulation. The market primarily includes two types: water heating and electric heating systems. Water heating systems utilize pipes installed in the ceiling, circulating warm water to radiate heat, making them energy-efficient and suitable for larger spaces. Electric heating systems, on the other hand, use electric wires or panels embedded in the ceiling to generate warmth quickly, offering easier installation and more precise temperature control.</p></p>
