Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed soft tissue visualization, crucial for identifying inflammation and other complications. Computer Tomography (CT) provides rapid imaging, useful in emergency settings for detecting potential intracranial issues. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) plays a role in assessing metabolic activity and differentiating infection from other brain disorders. Together, these technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning for meningitis patients.</p></p>
